Welcome to the Food Recall Impact map. Here you will find information collected by the Food and Drug Administration for recalled foods over the past 5 years.

The data may be filtered by food category, affected date, years, severity, and/or status.

We encourage you to use this data to educate and inform your community on recalled foods both past and present in your area.

For details on a particular recall, click on a pin. States affected by the recall will be highlighted.

If you have any questions, please refer to the About page which has answers to Frequently Asked Questions.
